# Cyberpunk GM Playbook

## Strong Ingredients
- a human face attached to an ugly system
- one tempting payoff and one hidden cost
- brand identity, city texture, and corporate propaganda
- consequences that create more play instead of dead ends

## Good Mission Seeds
- extract someone who does not want to be rescued
- steal data that turns out to be alive
- protect a leak long enough to publish it
- erase a debt ledger that is wired into real bodies
