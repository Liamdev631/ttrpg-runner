# Cyberpunk Flavor Pack

Load this pack only when the player explicitly wants a cyberpunk game.

## Tone Targets
- intimate human need under institutional violence
- seductive technology with ugly tradeoffs
- style under pressure
- debt, surveillance, scarcity, and retaliation

## Opening Frame
- Give the character room to act before the world acts on them.
- Do not start with multiple factions already mobilized against the crew.
- Pressure starts low and ramps because of player choices, not before them.

## Organic Creation Principle
- Invent fresh jobs, neighborhoods, corps, rumors, and betrayals every session.
- Use this pack's markdown references for tone and shape only, never as a random content vending machine.
- Keep the city responsive and personal to the player's concept.

## Stat Guidance
- `BOD` - force, endurance, intimidation
- `REF` - shooting, driving, dodging, precision
- `TEK` - hacking, repair, code surgery
- `INT` - planning, medicine, research
- `COO` - stealth, nerve, composure
- `CHA` - persuasion, seduction, performance

Suggested derived trackers:
- `HP = BOD + 4`
- `Defense = REF`
- `Stress = COO`
- `Reputation` starts at `0`

## Ambient Inserts
Reuse `templates/ad-crawl.md` for billboards, transit loops, bar screens, drone ads, scam messages, and rumor feeds. Roughly one in three should plant a hook the player could chase.

## Pressure Levers
- corp security and deniable violence
- gangs, labor unrest, and bad neighborhoods
- tracebacks, leaked footage, and surveillance
- debt collectors, clinics, and compromised implants

## Discord Rendering
- Lean on `-#` subtext for the time, weather, and "the city at this hour" ambient tag.
- Use `> ` block quotes for street voices, intercepted messages, and NPC dialogue. The block quote is the city talking back.
- Use `||...||` spoilers for corp secrets, hidden affiliations, and surveillance the player should be able to choose not to see.
- Italicize brand and street names with `*...*` so neon reads as neon.
- Bold the **fixer**, the **job**, and the **deadline** in every scene description.
- Do not render price lists, contacts, or inventory as Discord tables. Use a short bullet list or a fenced code block.
