# Expanse Gameplay Loop

1. Frame a material problem: transport, debt, damage, access, or political pressure.
2. Show what limited resource is actually on the line.
3. Let the crew solve it with engineering, leverage, stealth, or nerve.
4. Roll when vacuum, violence, acceleration, or politics become uncertain.
5. Escalate fallout through scarcity, faction heat, or human fracture.
6. Record ship status, contracts, enemies, and discoveries in the session files.
