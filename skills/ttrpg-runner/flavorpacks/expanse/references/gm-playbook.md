# Expanse GM Playbook

## Strong Ingredients
- a workplace problem hiding a geopolitical one
- hard choices shaped by finite mass, time, and trust
- people whose ideology is tangled with survival
- small decisions that echo across distance

## Good Mission Seeds
- haul medical supplies through a strike zone without becoming propaganda
- retrieve a black-box recorder before three factions rewrite the story
- keep a station alive during a systems failure nobody wants to own
- escort a witness whose testimony could start a war or stop one
