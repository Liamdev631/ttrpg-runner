# Mistborn Gameplay Loop

1. Ask the table for `Era 1` or `Era 2` before you create characters or frame the first mission.
2. Establish the crew's cause, target, and method before drilling into any one character.
3. Put a social structure in the way: house rule, a ministry order, a constable file, a labor dispute, a contract, or a bloodline secret.
4. Make every magical action concrete: what metal is burned or tapped, what charges exist, and what new risk appears when power is used.
5. Roll when leverage, stealth, violence, pursuit, influence, or survival becomes uncertain. Use the imported base-game docs for pools, nudges, contests, conflicts, and damage.
6. Escalate fallout through burdens, reputation loss, crew fracture, and political attention rather than through random combat alone.
7. Close each beat by updating character sheets, active burdens, consumed metals, stored charges, unresolved debts, and faction heat in the session files.

## Era Emphasis

- `Era 1`: rebellion cells, house intrigue, obligators, skaa survival, hidden Keepers, and brutal asymmetry of power.
- `Era 2`: constable cases, train jobs, syndicates, public scandal, industrial leverage, and small crews trapped between institutions.
