# Mistborn GM Playbook

## Strong Ingredients

- hard bargains made under unequal power
- small crews trying to outplan larger institutions
- magic that solves one problem by creating another
- burdens that change the next scene instead of being forgotten
- status, scarcity, and lineage as active pressures

## Good Mission Seeds

- steal or destroy evidence before a rival faction turns it into leverage
- protect a witness whose bloodline matters more than they understand
- retrieve a metalmind, spike, ledger, or contract before a house or syndicate does
- turn a public event into cover for a private betrayal

## Era 1 Pressure

- noble house feuds, skaa resistance, and Steel Ministry attention
- ash, hunger, curfews, and violent enforcement of class lines
- hidden Keepers, forbidden records, and dangerous bloodlines
- open magical power that can terrify a district when revealed

## Era 2 Pressure

- constable scrutiny, newspapers, rail choke points, and financial leverage
- syndicates, industrialists, political blocs, and public scandal
- firearms, city density, and collateral damage in crowded spaces
- Twinborn edge cases, inherited secrets, and old-empire artifacts resurfacing

## Best Practices

- Keep the crew central. Houses, ministries, constables, and syndicates matter because they squeeze the crew.
- Ask what metal is active whenever magic becomes important.
- Let success stand, but make the social and political consequences ripple.
- Use imported Era 1 institution docs carefully when the table is in Era 2.
